Why Donate ?

As an effort to help our country rebuild after the devastation caused by Hurricane Maria, we have created this platform to gather funds internationally. We are counting on the cooperation of the people worldwide to help us get through these tough times.

Hurricane Maria ripped through Puerto Rico on Wednesday, September 20th, making landfall as a powerful Category 4 storm with 155-mph winds, according to the National Hurricane Center, making it the most devastating hurricane that has crossed Puerto Rico in modern history. It left 100% of the island without power. In many places, there’s still no water to drink or bathe in or to flush toilets. Many people have died, and even more are missing. There are limited food and cell service, and dozens of remote villages have been completely cut off from everything for weeks. Our island is in desperate need of help. By donating, you'll be contributing to the immediate and long-term relief of Puerto Rico.

An estimated 100,000 homes and buildings were demolished in the storm, and 90 percent of the island's infrastructure was damaged or destroyed.

According to Quartz, as of December 24, the death toll stood at 64. About 1,000 more bodies were creamted after the hurricane without verification.

Approximately 80 percent of the territory's agriculture was lost due to the hurricane, with agricultural losses estimated at $780 million.

The hurricane completely destroyed the island's power grid, leaving all 3.4 million residents of the island without electricity.

Moody’s Analytics, a financial services firm, estimates the hurricane could cost Puerto Rico $45 billion to $90 billion.

The island’s communications infrastructure was badly damaged, leaving Puerto Rico and its residents almost entirely disconected from each other and the rest of the world.

Why Bitcoin ?

We believe thay on events as damaging as the one that Puerto Rico has recently suffered, we must find as many ways possible to bring funds for aid to our country. In order to do that, we must reach the entire world for help. Bitcoin and the blockchain offer great advantages for running an international crowdfunding effort such as this one. Some of those advantages are:

  • Transparency: Cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin operate in an open ledger, which is to say, every Bitcoin transaction ever can be seen by anyone. This means that every donor can be sure that their money is going to the right place and being used responsibly.
  • Fees: Another benefit of running a crowdfunding effort with Bitcoin is its low overhead. Unlike traditional banking and online payment systems, which can charge quite a high transaction, platform and currency exchange fees, Bitcoin allows transactions globally with very little cost. This means that more of your donation will actually reach the intended recipient
  • Accesibility: ​While traditional crowdfunding platforms may carry out disbursement cycles of up to weeks at a time, desperately needed funds might not make it to the recipients quickly enough. Fortunately, funds transferred on the blockchain are immediately accessible no matter how far they were transferred from.
  • Growing User Base: Thanks to the growing adoption of Bitcoin worldwide, we see a great opportunity to further expand the areas in which its core features can present a direct and immediate impact on the world. We believe this is a great application for the technology and we make a request to the Bitcoin society to help our country recover and rebuild. ​
