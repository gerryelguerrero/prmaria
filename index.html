Island Recovery Plans & Engagement

This document provides a high level and non-exhaustive overview of the issues and initiatives that have arisen in response to the impact of hurricanes Maria and Irma on Puerto Rico. The efforts of relief, recovery, and resiliency are often overlapping and should not be looked at as a sequential guide.

Initiatives
Below are a list of the initiatives by category. See what people are doing, and find out the best way you can help.

The provision of emergency services and public assistance during or immediately after a disaster in order to save lives, reduce health impacts, ensure public safety and meet the basic subsistence needs of the people affected.

The restoration, and improvement where appropriate, of facilities, livelihoods and living conditions of disaster-affected communities, including efforts to reduce disaster risk factors.

The ability of a system, community or society exposed to hazards to resist, absorb, accommodate to and recover from the effects of a hazard in a timely and efficient manner, including through the preservation and restoration of its essential basic structures and functions.

What are people doing ?

We have partnered with the most trustworthy and recognizable non-profit organizations dedicated to the immediate and long term relief efforts in Puerto Rico. Here is a list of the organizations we are currently working with:

ConPRmetidos

ConPRmetidos mission is to connect people to foster a commitment to the personal, social, and economic development of Puerto Rican communities, wherever they are. As one of the leading independent, non-partisan, non-profit economic empowerment NGOs in the island, no other time has our mission been so relevant. Please help us help power up Puerto Rico.
Learn More

Foundation for Puerto Rico

Foundation for Puerto Rico is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization whose mission is to transform Puerto Rico into a destination for the world by driving economic and social development through sustainable strategies.
Learn More

Flamboyan Foundation

Flamboyan and its founders have been working in Puerto Rico with nonprofit organizations, government agencies, media outlets, philanthropists, and businesses for over 25 years. They have partnered with teams on the island to support the immediate humanitarian and recovery efforts critical for the people of Puerto Rico. In the long-term, they remain committed to ensuring the island’s children thrive by advocating for, supporting, and restoring the island’s educational systems.
Learn More

Power4PR

Power4PR's mission is to provide power and lights to help families in the dark during this black out period. For every donation received, disposable chargers and lanterns will be provided to give Puerto Rico provisional lighting and power to charge their phones in order for them to be able to stay connected with their families and friends and operate in the darkness. For every product purchased, we will match and donate the same to Puerto Rico.
Learn More
